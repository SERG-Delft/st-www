Software Technology

The Department of Software Technology (ST) covers the design, engineering and analysis of complex, distributed and cooperative software and computer systems. Together with the Intelligent Systems Department (INSY), the department is responsible for Computer Science education and research at the Delft University of Technology.

Information on education, see the Bachelor (in Dutch) and Master program.

Bachelor programMaster program

The inspiration for our research topics is largely derived from technical ICT problems in industry and society with respect to large-scale distributed processing, embedded systems, programming productivity, and web-based information analysis and retrieval. These areas are reflected in our research programs.
